# Skillora – Connecting Skilled Workers to Global Opportunities

**Skillora** is a social impact platform designed to connect skilled workers in developing regions with global employment opportunities — promoting **fair wages**, **decent working conditions**, and **economic empowerment** through transparent, ethical recruitment.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between **talent and opportunity**, ensuring that workers from underrepresented regions can access fair global markets without exploitation.

We aim to:
- Promote **equitable wages** and **safe workplaces**
- Empower **local communities** through sustainable income
- Enable **global employers** to discover verified skilled professionals
- Foster **digital inclusion** and **ethical recruitment**

---

## Key Features

- **Global Job Matching:** AI-based system connects workers to opportunities based on skill, experience, and location.  
- **Skill Verification:** Workers can showcase verified certificates, portfolios, and ratings.  
- **Transparent Communication:** In-app messaging and employer reviews ensure transparency.  
- **Fair Wage Benchmarking:** Wage calculator ensures compensation aligns with regional and international standards.  
- **Worker Protection Policies:** Partnerships with NGOs and labor rights groups to promote ethical practices.  
- **Multilingual Access:** Built for inclusivity with support for multiple regional languages.
